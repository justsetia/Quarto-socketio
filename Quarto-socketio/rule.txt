 There are 8 differences :
 
Size ->  Tall : T    Short : K
Color -> White : W    Black : B
Shape -> Circle : C  Square : S
Content -> Full : F   Empty : E

A player calls "QUARTO !" and wins the game when, as he places the piece given to him:

He creates a line of 4 white pieces or 4 black pieces or 4 circle pieces or 4 square pieces or 
4 tall pieces or 4 short pieces or 4 full pieces or 4 empty pieces.

Different characteristics can accumulate.

He does not need to have placed the 3 other pieces himself.

He must declare his win by calling "QUARTO !"
